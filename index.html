Who's Herbal?

Herbalism refers to the use of plants or plant parts for medicinal or health purposes. Different With synthetic medicines, herbs tend to use natural ingredients that have been used for centuries by various cultures around the world. Along with that, research continues to be conducted to evaluate The safety and effectiveness of herbal use in a medical context.
